About us

"100% the best Tacos in Ireland! - Lovin Dublin"

Who We Are

Oaxaca Taco bar is run by husband and wife team, Jose and Maria Rodriguez who are both from Oaxaca in Mexico. They are passionate about Mexican food and most importantly love Tacos. They are deteremined to bring a taste of home to Dublin city.

You will find Maria at the front of the house always serving customers with a smile. Jose is the head chef who has spent years trying to perfect the Taco.

Our Story

After leaving home we decided to travel all over Europe until we visited Ireland and decided to call it our new home. We noticed how poor the quality of Mexican restaurants were and how hard it was to get a perfect Taco. We set out to bring all our knowledge of creating authenthic tacos.
